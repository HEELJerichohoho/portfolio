Safepal: School Security with Wearable GPS Tracking and Emergency Alerts

Screenshot of the Safepal system

Demo video of Safepal in action

The Safepal project is a school security system that integrates a wearable device with GPS tracking and an emergency alert function to ensure student safety. The device allows parents and school staff to monitor students’ real-time location through an Android application, while an emergency button provides instant notifications during critical situations. Developed using Agile Scrum methodology, Safepal emphasizes usability, reliability, and security, offering a practical technological solution to enhance safety measures within and beyond school premises.
